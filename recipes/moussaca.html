Moussaca Recipe Easy to Prepare.

This moussaca recipe is easy to prepare. The original can be found in the website allrecipes.com when searching for moussaka.

Layers of sliced eggplants are baked in a ground beef sauce and smothered in a creamy white bechamel sauce in this classic Greek disk.

The ingredients

  • 3 eggplants, peeled and cut lengthwise into 1/" inch thick slices
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4cup olive oli
  • tablespoon butter
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon fines herbs
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg, divided
  • 1 (8 ounces) can tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 4 cups milk 1/2 cup butter 6 tablespoons all-purpuse flour ground white pepper, to taste 1 1/" cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese

The Necessary Steps

  1. Slice egg plants. Warm olive oil in skillet over high heat. Fry eggplant until browned, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels if necessary.
  2. Fry the meat. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in ground beef, onions, and garlic; season with salt and black pepper. Cook and stir until the beeb is browned, 8 to 10 min.
  3. In the meet skillet add parsley, fine herbs, cinnamon, and 1/4 teaspoon nutmeag. Pour in tomato sauceand wine; mix well. Simmer for 20 minutes. Allow to cook and then stir in beaten egg.
  4. In a small pot prepare de bechamel sauce. Melt 1/2 cup of butter over medium heat. Whisk flour into butter until smooth. Lower heat; gradually pour milk, whisking constantly until it thickens. Season bechamel sauce with salt and white pepper.
  5. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C. Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish
  6. Layer the baking dish. Arange a single layer of eggplant in the prepared baking dish. Cover the eggplant with meat sauce, then sprinkle 1/2 cup Parmesan cheeese on top. Cover with remaining eggplant and sprinkle another 1/" cup cheese on top. Pour bechamel sauce on top and sprinkle with remaining 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg. Cover with remaining cheese.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven unitl bubbly and browned, about 45 min 1 hour.
  8. Let the baking dish rest for some minutes. Then serve it.
